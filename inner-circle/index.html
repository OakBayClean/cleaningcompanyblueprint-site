The reason we built this

Built on a different approach.

Vic and Jen built Oak Bay Clean to $2.8M in four years during the pandemic, after stumbling across a free Twitter course by Rohan Gilkes. A year later, Kevin from ConvertLabs and Rohan asked them to share what they'd learned on YouTube. The 21 Days to Launch series went up free as a way to pay it forward.

The more they shared, the clearer it got that their version of the model ran differently than the loud playbook most cleaning business coaches teach. Cleaners first, organic leads, SEO, and clients who respect the people doing the work.

The Inner Circle is the room where that approach lives.

The room

Four phases. One room. A mentor one stage ahead of you.

When you join, you're placed in your phase. Every phase has owners at the same stage, and a mentor one stage ahead. Everyone's in the full room for the weekly call, and in your phase group for the work that's specific to where you are.

Phase 1

The Leap

$0 to $10K/month

Pre-launch or newly launched. You're setting prices, picking tools, chasing your first recurring client. The wall here is analysis paralysis.

Your mentors: Nakita, Adam, and Ash
Phase 2

The Engine

$10K to $25K/month

The model is proven. You're exhausted from doing everything yourself. The wall is tuning the engine: fixing the systems so you spend your time on what actually matters.

Your mentor: Luke
Phase 3

The Boring Phase

$25K to $50K/month

It's working and it's steady. The wall here is boredom disguised as ambition, and the instinct to reinvent a wheel that's already rolling.

The Other Side

$50K+/month

You built this to buy back your time and your money. You have both. The wall is choosing what's next. Scale to $100K a month? Sell the company? Run it in under an hour a day and actually live the freedom? This phase is about what comes next when you finally have the time and the money to decide.

Your mentors: Vic and Jen

Vic and Jen also mentor every phase. Every member has access to both in the full weekly room.

A typical month

Weekly Zooms. Monthly themes. Real talk.

Weekly Zoom, Wednesdays at noon Pacific

Same room, same people every week. Members across Canada, the US, UK, Australia, and South Africa.

Monthly themes

One topic per month: Leads, Hiring, SEO, and so on. Worksheets, guest experts, and member case studies focused on that theme.

Hot Seats

Once a month, one member sits in the chair and Vic interviews them about their business. Everyone else watches and asks.

Guest experts

Monthly sessions with specialists on SEO, ads, hiring, bookkeeping, and operations who actually work with cleaning companies.

Quarterly goal setting

First week of every quarter: three goals and the one metric that tells you if you're on track.

The Struggle Bus

Members submit questions in advance and the mentors answer the ones that help the most people in the room.

Private community

Searchable library of every Hot Seat, guest session, and worksheet, plus a member directory.
