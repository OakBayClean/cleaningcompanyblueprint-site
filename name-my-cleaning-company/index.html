Four things in one free report, specific to your city.

Market verdict

Strong, Moderate, or Tough, pulled from real local data. Know before you spend a dollar on a business name or logo whether the opportunity is actually there.

Competitor teardown

Which local cleaning companies take online bookings and show their prices. Knowing who's already set up properly tells you exactly where your opening is.

Ranked business names

Names generated for your market with .com availability checked live at the time of your report. No guessing, no checking manually, no discovering later that the domain is gone.

What to charge for a standard bi-weekly clean in your area, based on what's visible in your local market. A starting point grounded in real data, not a number pulled from thin air.
