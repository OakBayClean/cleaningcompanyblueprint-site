# Why Building Your Business in Community is Easier

Nakita explains why growth feels easier when you’re not building alone

There was a moment during the Hot Seat with Nakita where everything clicked.

She described the shift from riding the struggle bus to designing her business around what actually matters — and how simplifying created real momentum (and real profit).
