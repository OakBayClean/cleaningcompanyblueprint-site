The story

Built on a different approach.

Vic and Jen built Oak Bay Clean to $2.8M in four years during the pandemic, after stumbling across a free Twitter course by Rohan Gilkes. A year later, Kevin from ConvertLabs and Rohan asked them to share what they'd learned. The YouTube channel went up free as a way to pay it forward.

Everything CCB does runs on that same approach: cleaners first, free content, prices someone just starting can afford, and a room that takes care of the people in it.
