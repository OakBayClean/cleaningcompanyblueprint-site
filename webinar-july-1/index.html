What you'll walk away with

The actual playbook, stage by stage.

Most cleaning business advice online treats the business like one thing. It isn't. It's four. The wall at $5K is not the wall at $25K, and the wall at $50K is not the wall at $25K. Trying to use one playbook across all four stages is why most owners stall. In 90 minutes, you'll get the version that adapts to where you actually are.

You'll leave knowing exactly which stage you're in, what the wall looks like in your stage, what the next stage requires, and what you should stop doing right now if you want to get there. You'll also see the way Vic and Jen run the room, including what makes the Inner Circle different from every other coaching offer in this space.

The framework

Four stages. The wall at each one.

Each stage in the webinar comes with the wall most owners hit there, the change in mindset that gets you through, and the specific actions that move the needle. You'll see real examples from real businesses inside the Inner Circle.

Stage 1

The Leap

$0 to $10K/month

Pre-launch or newly launched. You're setting prices, picking tools, chasing your first recurring client. The wall is analysis paralysis: you have too many options and not enough motion. The breakthrough is picking one thing and committing for 90 days.

Stage 2

The Engine

$10K to $25K/month

The model is proven. You're exhausted from doing everything yourself. The wall is tuning the engine: fixing the systems so you spend your time on what actually matters. The breakthrough is letting go of the things you're best at, in service of the things only you can do.

Stage 3

The Boring Phase

$25K to $50K/month

It's working and it's steady. The wall here is boredom disguised as ambition, and the instinct to reinvent a wheel that's already rolling. The breakthrough is keeping your hands off and trusting the system you built.

Stage 4

The Other Side

$50K+/month

You built this to buy back your time and your money. You have both. The wall is choosing what's next. Scale to $100K? Sell? Run it under an hour a day and live the freedom? This stage is about making that choice on purpose.
