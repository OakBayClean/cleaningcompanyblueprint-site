A few quick details

What to expect.

How long is the call?

30 minutes. We start on time and end on time.

Is there really no charge?

Yes, the discovery call is free. The paid 1:1 calls are $250 an hour and are booked separately through stan.store/CleaningCompanyBlueprint. The discovery call exists so you can decide if a paid call, the Inner Circle, or something else is the right fit before spending money.

Who runs the call?

Vic. All discovery calls are 1:1 with Vic. If your question is specifically about branding, websites, or SEO (Jen's wheelhouse), Vic will route you to Jen for a paid 1:1 if it makes sense after the discovery call.

What's on the booking form?

A few short questions about your situation so Vic can prep before the call: where you are with your business, what you're hoping to figure out, what your goals look like, and what's been holding you back. The form takes about three minutes. It's there so the 30 minutes on Zoom go straight to the useful conversation instead of the catch-up.

Should I prep anything else?

Show up with the actual question on your mind. If you have specific numbers about your business (revenue, hours, current clients), have those handy. If you don't, that's fine too.

What happens after the call?

If something we offer is the right fit, we'll point you to it. If something else is the right fit, we'll tell you that instead. There's no automated email sequence, no high-pressure follow-up. We treat these calls as the kind of conversation we'd have with someone who reached out for help, not as a sales funnel.

Can I reschedule or cancel?

Yes. The confirmation email has a reschedule and a cancel link. Use them anytime. Just please don't no-show, since the slots fill up and someone else could have used it.
